# NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)

## Govern
Cultivates and implements a culture of risk management within organizations designing, developing, deploying, or acquiring AI systems. Maps to organizational values, principles, and policies.

## Map
Context is established and risks related to an AI system are identified, described, and analyzed. Includes understanding the system's intended purpose, domain, and stakeholder impact.

## Measure
Employs tools, techniques, and methodologies to analyze, assess, benchmark, and evaluate AI risk and related impacts. Includes quantitative and qualitative approaches.

## Manage
Involves allocating risk resources to mapped and measured risks on a regular basis and as defined by an organization's risk tolerance. Includes responding to, recovering from, and communicating about risk events.
