# Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA) and Fair Lending

## Prohibited Bases
ECOA prohibits discrimination in any aspect of a credit transaction based on race, color, religion, national origin, sex, marital status, age, or because an applicant receives income from a public assistance program.

## Adverse Action Notices
Creditors must provide applicants with specific reasons for adverse action, and the reasons must be specific and indicate the principal reason(s) for the adverse action.

## Disparate Impact in Lending
Lending practices that have a disproportionately negative impact on a protected class, even if the practice appears neutral, may violate ECOA unless the practice is justified by business necessity and there is no less discriminatory alternative.
