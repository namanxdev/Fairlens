# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Enterprise compliance teams, AI auditors, data scientists, and ML engineers who need to ensure their models meet regulatory standards (like the EU AI Act or NIST RMF).

## Product Purpose
FairLens is an AI bias detection and audit platform. It allows users to upload datasets, profile data for protected attributes, run statistical fairness metrics, and generate compliance-ready audit reports.

## Brand Personality
Serious, trust-oriented, high-end, technical, and precise. It embraces a "Vercel-core" aesthetic—clean, dark-themed, and highly professional.

## Anti-references
- "AI Slop" (generic purple/blue glowing gradients)
- Playful, toy-like or bubbly aesthetics (no emojis)
- Predictable symmetrical layouts (e.g., exactly centered hero text, 3 equal feature cards)
- Generic startup templates (e.g., using "Inter" font everywhere)

## Design Principles
- **Expert Confidence:** The UI should look like a precision instrument, not a toy.
- **Asymmetric Balance:** Use asymmetry to create visual interest and hierarchy, avoiding default centered layouts.
- **Micro-interactions matter:** Use refined, physics-based motion (like liquid glass refraction or magnetic hover) that feels deliberate.
- **Data Forward:** Visualizations and stats should look crisp and legible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability in dark mode.
- Clear typographic hierarchy for scanning complex audit data.
- Accessible interactive elements (focus states, clear hit areas).
